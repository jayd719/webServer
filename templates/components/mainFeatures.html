How does Klarna work?

We partner with top retailers all over the world to make it easy to split your purchase into manageable payments. The first payment is made at the checkout and the remaining payments are collected every 2 weeks.

At the checkout

Select the option to pay with Klarna and enter your payment information. There are no fees when payments are made on time. A higher initial payment may be required for some purchases.

When the order is shipped

Once your order is confirmed and on it’s way, we’ll charge the first installment. Once the payment has been processed you will receive a notification.

Every two weeks

We will automatically charge your card every two weeks for the remaining three payments. We'll send you an alert ahead of time.
